I am currently a research intern at NYU and a Machine Learning Researcher and Co-Founder at Cyrion Labs. My work focuses on human-computer interaction, visual language models, bias, and applied AI.

Previously, I contributed to scalable systems and AI-driven platforms in roles ranging from Developer to CTO in startups and established companies. In my free time, I volunteer as a computer refurbishing technician making computers more accessible for the visually impaired.
